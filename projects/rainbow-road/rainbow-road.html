Rainbow Road redone in the original Mario Party. Obviously, there are no Chain Chomps and the standard rule of getting a moving Star for 20 coins applies. Features two Boo locations, one Bowser, and one Koopa Troopa passing point. Happening Spaces do not work. Landing on them does nothing, but it does add to your total for the Happening Bonus Star.
